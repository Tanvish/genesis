About us

"Biology is the science, Biology is the evolution."

The Biology Club at DPS Dwarka has been assimilated to give our young students an opportunity to delve into the diverse world of biology. The Club over heads biological experiment around the school, and it give the students the opportunity to gather information about the biological world around them, thus providing them a wider insight on the subject. In future the club promises exciting events that will be other interesting and educational.
